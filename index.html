Ring of Fire rules

  1. Select number of participants
  2. Input names for all participants
  3. Click start game

In Ring of Fire, every player draws a card in order, going clockwise.
Cards all have different rules and actions which must be performed after that card has been drawn. Here is a list of actions for each card:

  • 2's: Two is You! Give another player 3 sips.
  • 3's: Three is Me! You have to drink 3 sips.
  • 4's: Four is floor! Last player to point their finger to the floor drinks 3 sips.
  • 5's: Five is Guys! Everyone who identifies as a guy drinks 3 sips.
  • 6's: Six is Chicks! Everyone who identifies as a chick drinks 3 sips.
  • 7's: Seven to Heaven! Last player to point their finger to the sky drinks 3 sips.
  • 8's: Eight is Mate! Choose another player to be your drinking buddy. For every sip you take, they must take one as well!
  • 9's: Nine is Rhyme! The player who drew the card starts by saying a word. Going clockwise, every player must then say a word that rhymes with the chosen words. First player to fail or hesitate for too long takes 5 sips!
  • 10's: Categories! Name a category (e.g. dog breeds, car brands etc). Starting with the player who drew the card and going clockwise, every player names a word in that category until someones fails or hesitates for too long. Whoever fails first takes 5 sips!
  • Jacks: Never Have I Ever! Each player raises 3 fingers. Starting with the player who drew the card and going clockwise, say a "Never have I ever" statement. If any player has done what the statement says, that player lowers a finger. First player to lower all 3 fingers takes 5 sips!
  • Queens: Question Master! You are now the Question Master until another player draws a Queen. Any time another player answers one of your questions, that player must drink 3 sips.
  • Kings: King's cup! The player who draws this card pours a bit of their drink in a cup. The last player to draw a King must drink whatever is in the cup.
  • Aces: Rule maker! Decide a rule that all players (including yourself) must follow. If anyone breaks or does not follow the rule(s), that player takes 3 sips.
